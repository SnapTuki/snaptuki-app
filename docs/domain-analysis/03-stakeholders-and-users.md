# Stakeholders and Users

## Primary Users

### Care Coordinator

Responsible for creating, assigning, monitoring, and following up care tasks.

Needs:
- Create care tasks.
- Assign tasks to caregivers.
- Monitor delayed or overdue tasks.
- Support shift handover.
- React when assistance is needed.

### Caregiver

Responsible for carrying out assigned care tasks.

Needs:
- See assigned tasks clearly.
- Understand resident, room, due time, and instructions.
- Mark tasks as completed, delayed, or needing assistance.
- Add short notes.

### Manager

Responsible for operational visibility, quality monitoring, and accountability.

Needs:
- See daily task progress.
- Identify overdue or delayed care.
- Review reports.
- Understand incident and task trends.

### Admin

Responsible for managing users, roles, residents, rooms, and facility settings.

## Secondary Stakeholders

### Resident

Benefits from more consistent, timely, and safer care.

### Family Member

May later receive approved updates, depending on privacy rules.

### Investor

Needs to understand the product’s market value, scalability, and business model.
