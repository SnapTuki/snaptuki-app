# Subdomains

## Core Subdomains

### Care Task Coordination

The main MVP subdomain. Handles creation, assignment, completion, delay, assistance, and monitoring of care tasks.

### Care Routine Management

Handles recurring care routines, templates, and resident-specific routine schedules.

### Shift Handover

Handles information transfer between shifts, including unfinished tasks, notes, and residents needing attention.

### Incident Handling

Handles incident reporting, severity, review, follow-up actions, and closure.

### Care Safety Monitoring

Handles patterns such as repeated missed routines, frequent incidents, risky locations, and overdue task trends.

## Supporting Subdomains

### Resident Profile Management

Maintains basic resident information needed for care coordination.

### Facility and Room Management

Models facility, units, floors, rooms, and locations.

### Workload Visibility

Shows workload by caregiver, resident, unit, or shift.

### Reporting

Provides task reports, pilot reports, and quality summaries.

### Device Management

Manages QR/NFC tags, room devices, and physical reporting devices.

### Family Communication

Allows approved updates to family members in later phases.

## Generic Subdomains

### Authentication

Login, password reset, session management, and SSO.

### User Account Management

Creating, editing, and deactivating users.

### Notification Delivery

Email, SMS, push notifications, and delivery retries.

### Audit Logging Infrastructure

Technical logging of actions and changes.

### File Storage

Attachments, exports, and reports.

### Integration Infrastructure

APIs, webhooks, tokens, and external system communication.
