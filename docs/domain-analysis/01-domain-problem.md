# Domain Problem

## Domain

Nursing Home Care Operations and Safety Coordination

## Problem Statement

Nursing homes manage many care-related activities every day. Care coordinators, caregivers, nurses, and managers must coordinate tasks across residents, rooms, shifts, 
and priorities.

Mistakes can happen when care tasks are unclear, responsibilities are not assigned, shift handovers are incomplete, or incidents are reported too late.

Snaptuki exists to make daily care work visible, assignable, trackable, and reportable.

## Core Problem

How can a nursing home ensure that daily care tasks are clearly assigned, completed on time, monitored by coordinators, and documented for accountability?
How can a nursing home ensure that their residents receive reliable care, treated professionally?
How can a nursing home ensure that incidents are reported fast enough and actions taken accordingly?

## Problems Observed

- Care tasks are missed or delayed.
- Caregivers may not know who is responsible for what.
- Managers lack real-time visibility.
- Shift handovers can lose important information.
- Incidents are reported late or incompletely.
- Resident-specific routines may not be visible at the point of care.
- Documentation is fragmented.
- Resident receives wrong medications
- Care routines are not transparent for residents' families

## Desired Outcome

Snaptuki should help nursing homes reduce avoidable mistakes by improving care task coordination, visibility, accountability, and incident follow-up.
