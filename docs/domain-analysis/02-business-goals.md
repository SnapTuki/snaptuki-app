# Business Goals

## Product Goal

Build a digital care coordination platform that helps nursing homes manage daily care work more reliably.

## Business Goals

1. Reduce missed or delayed care tasks.
2. Improve task ownership and accountability.
3. Give managers real-time visibility into care operations.
4. Improve shift handover quality.
5. Create better documentation for internal reviews.
6. Prepare a future foundation for incident handling.
7. Support safer and more consistent care for residents.

## MVP Goal

The MVP should prove that care coordinators can create and assign care tasks, caregivers can update task status, and managers can monitor progress.

## Long-Term Goal

Snaptuki should become a care operations and safety platform for nursing homes, including care task coordination, routines, handover, 
incident handling, devices, and analytics.
